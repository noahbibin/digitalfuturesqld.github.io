The future of technology will be amazing, from holograms to hover cars, this will change life forever!

We can predict that many things will change in the future. Some cars already have a self-driving function, scientists believe it is safer this way, allowing less accidents. And probably by 2050 many families will have home drones, which is basically a car that can fly and transport you to places quicker. School could also be very different. Right now, because of the COVID-19 pandemic many students must do school online on their iPad, laptop or desktop computer. In the future, school could be filled with smart computers and holograms, but they probably still be encouraging students to go to school to enhance their social skills.

In the future your dreams could be reality. This technology is being worked on in the present, like 3D printing food and make meat without animals to Space tourism and settling on planets like Mars. And scientists are sure that VR and AR technology will prove to be one of the biggest innovations for the future in going to your class or work to being able to play games without a TV, PC or Console. People are already trying to make a 5G network for people around the world for faster network speeds. Maybe you could visit Mars in your bedroom without a screen but with holograms. This will prove useful for people in the future for education. This is will happen in the future!
